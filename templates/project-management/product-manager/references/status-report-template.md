# Status Report Template

Use this template for executive status updates. Post as Jira epic comment.

---

## Format for Jira Comment

```markdown
## Status Update: {Epic Name}
**Date:** {YYYY-MM-DD}
**Reporter:** Product Manager

---

### Summary
{One sentence: Overall status and key highlight}

### Progress
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Stories Complete | {X} of {Y} ({%}) |
| In Progress | {X} |
| Blocked | {X} |

**Milestones:**
- [x] {Completed milestone}
- [ ] {Upcoming milestone} — ETA: {date}

### Blockers & Risks
{If none: "No blockers at this time."}

| Issue | Impact | Owner | Action |
|-------|--------|-------|--------|
| {Blocker} | {High/Med/Low} | {Name} | {Next step} |

### Key Decisions Needed
{If none: "No decisions pending."}

- {Decision 1} — Needed by: {date}

### Next Steps
1. {Action 1} — {Owner}
2. {Action 2} — {Owner}

---
*Generated by Product Manager skill*
```

---

## Quick Status Levels

Use these status indicators consistently:

| Status | Meaning | Emoji |
|--------|---------|-------|
| On Track | Progressing as planned | :green_circle: |
| At Risk | Issues that may cause delay | :yellow_circle: |
| Blocked | Cannot proceed without resolution | :red_circle: |
| Complete | All acceptance criteria met | :white_check_mark: |

---

## Executive Summary Format

For leadership updates, use this condensed format:

```
**{Epic Name}** — {Status Emoji} {Status}

Progress: {X}% complete ({done}/{total} stories)
ETA: {date}
Key Risk: {One-liner or "None"}
```

---

## Weekly Digest Format

For weekly team updates:

```markdown
## Week of {Date Range}

### Completed This Week
- {Story KEY}: {Summary}
- {Story KEY}: {Summary}

### In Progress
- {Story KEY}: {Summary} — {Owner}

### Planned Next Week
- {Story KEY}: {Summary}

### Blockers
- {Description} — Escalated to: {Name}
```
